Program Details

About:

She Hacks is an organization dedicated to teaching women front end web development in the most affordable and effective way possible. We teach you how to use programming as a tool to accomplish what you care about - not a foreign language you will never find relevant to your daily life. Throughout the program, you will create many websites and learn how to advertise to audiences. By the end, you will build your own portfolio to display your creations on. This program is totally free and real-life application based. Fill out the registration form to join us for the first lesson November 5th, 2020.

Dates and Times:

She Hacks will take place every Thursday from November 5th 2020 to February 5th 2020, with a total of 12 sessions. Each session will be 30 minutes long, from 6:30 to 7pm EST, with an optional 15 minute office hours after. The time zone is America/New_York.
